✦ Tips & Tricks

Master Your Online Memo Writing

Level up your note-taking with these proven strategies for using Memo NotePad Online efficiently.

01

Use Descriptive Titles

Give every memo a clear, specific title like "Project Alpha Meeting — May 2025" instead of "Notes". It makes searching 10× faster.

02

Start With a TL;DR

Begin each memo with a one-line summary. When you scan your notes list later, that summary instantly refreshes your memory.

03

Use Bold for Action Items

Make tasks pop by bolding them. When reviewing meeting memos, your action items jump out immediately from the reading.

04

Create Template Memos

Write a reusable meeting notes template. Duplicate it before each session to save setup time every single week.

05

Download Important Memos

For critical information, download a .txt backup. Local storage can be cleared — always have a copy of vital memos.

06

Keyboard Shortcut Workflow

Use Ctrl+B for bold, Ctrl+I for italic, Ctrl+U for underline. Format on the fly without lifting your hands from the keys.
