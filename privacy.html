Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 11, 2026

At Memo NotePad Online, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains how our application handles your data. Unlike traditional note-taking apps, we prioritize a "privacy-by-design" approach where your content remains under your control.

1. Data Storage

The most important thing to know is that your memos are never uploaded to our servers. All notes, titles, and formatting are stored locally on your own device using browser technology called LocalStorage.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

Because we do not require a login, we do not collect:

  • Your name, email address, or phone number.
  • The content of your memos or notes.
  • Personal identifiers that link you to your data.

3. Analytics and Cookies

We may use minimal, privacy-friendly analytics (such as Google Analytics) to understand general traffic patterns (e.g., how many people visit the site). These tools may use cookies to track anonymous usage data, such as:

  • Browser type and device category.
  • Referral source (how you found us).
  • Time spent on the site.

4. Third-Party Services

We use Google Fonts and FontAwesome to provide a high-quality visual experience. These services may process your IP address to deliver font files to your browser.

5. Data Security

Since your data is stored locally on your device, the security of your notes depends on the security of your device and browser. We recommend:

  • Not using this app on public or shared computers without clearing browser data afterward.
  • Regularly using our "Download" feature to back up important memos.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
