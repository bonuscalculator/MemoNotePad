📖 About This Digital Diary

My Diary is a modern, privacy-first online journal that lives entirely in your browser. Unlike cloud-based journaling apps, your entries never leave your device — they are stored securely in your browser's local storage. With mood tracking, weather tags, a visual calendar, and writing streak analytics, this diary transforms daily journaling into a mindful ritual.

🔐 Client-side encrypted 📅 Streak tracking 🎭 Mood & weather log 📤 JSON/TXT export 🌓 Dark mode 📱 Fully responsive

✨ Why choose My Diary? Because your memories and thoughts deserve a safe, beautiful, and distraction-free space. No ads, no tracking, no subscription. Just pure journaling joy.

📘 How to Use — Start Journaling in Minutes

1

Create Your First Entry

Select any date on the calendar, give your entry a title, and start writing in the rich-text editor. Add your mood and weather with the dropdowns.

2

Auto-save & Manage

Click "Save entry" to store your journal. All entries appear in the sidebar and calendar with a colored dot. Edit anytime — your history is preserved.

3

Lock & Secure (Optional)

Set a passphrase via "Lock diary". Your entries become protected behind a simple but private lock. Only you can unlock with the correct phrase.

4

Export & Backup

Use "Export all entries" to download a JSON backup. Import it later on any device. You can also manually copy text for offline archives.

💡 Pro tip: Keep a consistent streak! The calendar highlights days you've written. Try to write daily to extend your 🔥 streak number — a great motivation for mindful journaling.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

🔒 Is my diary truly private? +
Yes! All diary entries are stored only in your browser's local storage. Nothing is ever transmitted to any server. The optional passphrase lock adds an extra layer of protection — your journal stays on your device, 100% private.
📱 Can I use My Diary on my phone or tablet? +
Absolutely. The interface is fully responsive and touch-friendly. The editor, calendar, and sidebar adapt perfectly to smaller screens, so you can journal from anywhere — on the bus, during lunch, or before bed.
💾 What happens if I clear my browser data? +
Clearing browser cache or local storage will delete all diary entries. We highly recommend using the "Export all entries" feature regularly to keep a safe backup on your computer or cloud drive. Then you can re-import anytime.
🔥 How does the streak counter work? +
The streak shows your longest consecutive days with at least one journal entry. Write every day to increase your streak! Even a short sentence counts. It's designed to encourage consistency and habit formation.
🌈 Can I edit past entries? +
Yes. Simply click any date on the calendar or select an entry from the recent list. The editor will load that day's content. Edit, update mood/weather, and save again. Timestamps are automatically updated.
📤 What formats can I export? +
Currently, "Export all entries" gives you a JSON file containing all titles, dates, moods, weather, and rich-text content. You can also manually copy the text content of any entry to your clipboard. For plain text backup we recommend using browser developer tools or manually copy-paste.
🔄 Can I transfer my diary to another device? +
Yes! Use the "Export all entries" on device A to get the .json backup file. Transfer the file to device B (via email, cloud, USB), then use "Import backup" button on device B. Your entire journal will be restored.
