How the Decision Maker Works

This advanced tool provides three powerful random decision methods: an unbiased coin flip (Heads/Tails), a virtual six-sided dice roll, and a fully customizable spinner that lets you add your own choices. Each decision is logged to help track outcomes. Sound effects add excitement, and the result animations make each decision feel tangible. Perfect for resolving everyday dilemmas, game nights, team voting, or creative writing prompts. All decisions are generated locally — fast, private, and reliable.

Frequently Asked Questions

❓ Is the coin flip truly random?

Yes, it uses cryptographically strong pseudo-random generator (Math.random seeded with timestamp). Unbiased heads/tails simulation.

🎲 Can I roll multiple dice?

Currently single D6, but you can click repeatedly; history shows each roll. Perfect for board games.

✏️ How many custom choices can I add?

Up to 12 custom options. You can delete any you don't need. Weighted equally random.

🔊 Do sounds affect performance?

No, simple HTML5 audio beep simulation, optional toggle. No data sent. Turn off anytime.

📱 Does it work on mobile?

Fully responsive, touch-friendly buttons and inputs. Works on all modern browsers.

🕹️ Is history stored persistently?

History stores during session; clearing browser resets, but you can manually clear. 50 latest entries.
